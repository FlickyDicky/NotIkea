where are we located?


Not IKEA is located in the charming town of El Mojón, in the northern part of Lanzarote, Spain. This picturesque location provides a stunning backdrop for a furniture store that offers a unique selection of stylish and high-quality home decor. Whether you're a local resident or a visitor to the island, you'll find that not IKEA is a welcoming destination where you can shop for furniture and decor that reflects your personal style and enhances your living space. The warm and sunny climate of Lanzarote also adds to the overall shopping experience, making it a joy to browse our selection of furniture and decor.

Why?


We chose to locate not IKEA in El Mojón, Lanzarote for its unique charm and popularity as a tourist destination. The warm and sunny climate of the island also makes it an ideal location for a furniture store offering stylish and high-quality home decor options.
